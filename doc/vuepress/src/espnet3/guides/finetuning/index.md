# Finetuning

Fine-tuning in ESPnet3 usually means loading a checkpoint, changing
`training.yaml`, and then running `train`, `infer`, and `measure`.

- Load a pretrained checkpoint: [Load checkpoint](./load-checkpoint.md)
- Change training settings: [Config for finetuning](./config-for-finetuning.md)
- Freeze or limit trainable layers: [Freeze layers](./freeze-layers.md)
- Point to your own data: [Custom dataset](./custom-dataset.md)
- Evaluate and publish: [Evaluate and publish](./evaluate-and-publish.md)
